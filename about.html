Your Trusted Travel
Partner in Sri Lanka

At Diamond Athuruga, we specialize in providing reliable and comfortable vehicle services for foreign tour operators and individual travelers exploring the beauty of Sri Lanka. Whether you're planning a solo adventure or traveling with a group, we offer a range of transportation solutions tailored to your needs.

Our fleet includes well-maintained cars, vans, and buses, all operated by experienced drivers and knowledgeable guides, certified by the Sri Lanka Tourist Board. With four owned vehicles and access to an extensive network of trusted suppliers, we ensure seamless service during both peak and off-peak seasons. For larger groups or special tours, we collaborate with our suppliers to provide additional vehicles, guaranteeing flexibility and quality at all times.

Our drivers are not just trained professionals but also ambassadors of exceptional service, dedicated to making your journey safe, enjoyable, and unforgettable. We are also proud to partner with leading tour operators who entrust us with their guests, ensuring they experience the best of Sri Lanka.
